## Quality Assurance Management Commission ("CGAQ")

The Quality Assurance Management Commission ("CGAQ") operates at the Degree Program level to manage **self-assessment, review, and improvement activities**.

The Commission is responsible for drafting the **Annual Monitoring Sheet** (*Scheda di Monitoraggio Annuale*) and the **Cyclic Review Report** (*Rapporto di Riesame Ciclico*) of the Degree Program, operating in compliance with **European ESG standards**. The "CGAQ" includes professors, educational management staff, and a **student representation**.
