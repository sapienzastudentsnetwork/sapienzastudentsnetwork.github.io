{{% hint tip %}}
<i class="fa-solid fa-lightbulb" style="color: #238636;"></i> **Exam Sessions Not Showing on Infostud?**

If you are unable to register for an exam because it is not showing up in the Infostud system, **send a request to the Educational Affairs Office** at [segr.didattica@di.uniroma1.it](mailto:segr.didattica@di.uniroma1.it) to resolve the issue, following these steps:

* **Email Subject:** `Missing Exam in Infostud`
* **Email Content:** Make sure to clearly specify:
    * The **academic year of your first enrollment**
    * The **course name**
    * The **name of the professor** for the exam you wish to take
{{% /hint %}}
