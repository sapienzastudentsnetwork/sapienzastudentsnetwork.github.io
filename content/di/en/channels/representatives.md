# Student Representatives

The student representatives have the task of **collecting feedback, reports, and proposals** from the student body and **acting as their spokespersons** to the teaching staff, the course coordination, and the competent bodies.

These reports may include **critical issues, requests for clarification, or constructive proposals** to improve teaching and services. Anyone with reports, proposals, or needs in line with what is described is invited to **contact their representatives directly**.

Among the main activities carried out:
* **Listening to and collecting** reports, requests, and proposals from students, **identifying the most suitable channel to direct them to** (professors, secretariat, dean's office, etc.);
* **Collaborating** with other representatives present in other bodies (e.g., Department Council, Faculty Assembly, Committees, etc.) to **report issues or proposals** that go beyond the scope of a single degree program.
