## Joint Teacher-Student Committee ("CPDS")

The Joint Teacher-Student Committee established at the Faculty level (in our case, the Faculty of Information Engineering, Informatics, and Statistics - "I3S") is the **educational liaison structure** provided for by Law 240/2010.

It carries out activities of **monitoring the educational offering**, the **quality of teaching**, and the **services offered** to students by the teaching staff and researchers. Furthermore, the Committee **identifies the indicators for the evaluation** of results, formulates **opinions on the activation or suppression of degree programs**, and prepares an **Annual Report**.

For the student component, the "CPDS" includes one representative elected in the "I3S" Faculty Assembly for each department.
